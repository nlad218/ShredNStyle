## Usage
The Home page features all top projects a multiple links to other pages.

The Resort info will provide info on most resorts in the US and give current weather data for their locations.

The Passes section will show all available resort info with links to purchase.

The Shop page will show all available products by category.

The About section will show a brief bio for everyone on the team.

Once you log in or sign up, you have access to the cart section. It will show any items currently in your cart. Proceeding to checkout will prompt the user for a credit card (No charge takes place) and the user will be sent an email confirmation and clear out the cart.
